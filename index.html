About Kendara

Kendara is founded on the principles of Cognitive Utility Theory (CUT), a unique framework for understanding how intrinsic motivations drive human behavior. We believe that by understanding and nurturing our core drives, individuals can achieve a state of lasting well-being and contribute to a more harmonious world.

Our Vision

At Kendara, we envision a future where every individual is empowered to meet their intrinsic needs, leading to widespread personal flourishing. This foundation enables a compassionate approach to global well-being, fostering a truly harmonious and thriving world for all.

"From a CUT perspective, widespread well-being arises as individuals advance through their own feedback loops, naturally fostering societal benefit once personal drives are consistently fulfilled."
